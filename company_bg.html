1. Company Overview

Dynamic Traffic System Sdn Bhd (DynaTech)

Dynamic Traffic System Sdn Bhd was established in 2012.

Their mission is to maximize each client's competitive position by providing innovative solutions and value-added services in a way that exceeds their quality expectations.

The company's vision is to be the best manufacturer company in the eyes of their clients, communities, and people.

Location: The company is located at Seksyen BS6 Bukit Sentosa, 48300 Rawang, Selangor.

2. Core Business & Services

Dynamic Traffic System Sdn Bhd specializes in the design and manufacturing of traffic management products, including traffic light controllers and related systems. They focus on traffic light products and have expanded their offerings to include advanced technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT) solutions, RF Greenwave Link, and RF Countdown systems.

Their comprehensive services encompass custom traffic light solutions, professional installation and setup, 24/7 technical support, routine maintenance and repairs, system upgrades to incorporate the latest technology, and expert training and consultation to optimize traffic flow and product utilization.
