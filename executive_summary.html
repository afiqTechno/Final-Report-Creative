EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report provides a comprehensive overview of my 20-week industrial training at Dynamic Traffic System Sdn Bhd (DynaTech), conducted from January 5th to May 22nd, 2026. During this period, I was integrated into the AI depatment, focusing on two core domains: 3D environment simulation for traffic monitoring and modern Android application development.

Key Technical Contributions

My primary responsibility involved the Radar Digital Twin Integration project. I successfully developed a workflow to transform JKR geospatial data and satellite imagery into high-fidelity 3D junction models using the Unity engine. These models serve as a foundational spatial framework for future radar telemetry integration.

Additionally, I contributed to the modernization of the company's mobile software ecosystem. This included:

  • Developing UI components using Jetpack Compose and Kotlin.
  • Auditing and documenting system architectures, including ERDs and Use Case Diagrams.
  • Integrating backend services via PHP APIs and PostgreSQL databases.
  • Researching and implementing YOLO-based machine learning models for vehicle classification using datasets of over 21,000 images.

Professional Development

Beyond technical implementation, this internship facilitated growth in Version Control (Git/GitHub), and UI/UX design via Figma. I gained firsthand experience in the full software development lifecycle—from initial site visits and requirement gathering to deployment and hardware maintenance.

Conclusion

The training at DynaTech has bridged the gap between my academic studies at Politeknik Sultan Idris Shah and real-world industrial demands. Equipped with these practical skills and a professional mindset, I am confident in my ability to contribute effectively to the digital technology sector. I am deeply grateful to my supervisors and colleagues for their mentorship throughout this transformative experience.
